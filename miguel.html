Dr. Miguel Ángel Álvarez Carmona

Researcher appointed at CIMAT-Monterrey by CONAHCyT

SNI Level 1. In 2012, he received his Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering from BUAP, and in 2014 and 2019, he obtained his Master's and Doctorate degrees in Computer Science from INAOE, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing. He has authored various articles in national and international journals and conferences. Since 2019, he has been a researcher for Mexico at CONACyT in CIMAT-Monterrey Unit. He is a member of the Mexican Association of Natural Language Processing and the Mexican Society of Artificial Intelligence.
